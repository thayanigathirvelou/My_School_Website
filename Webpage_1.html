About School

Vigneswara College is a well-known institution at karaveddy, founded by virabaku sittampalam in 1917.It is one of the 105 High schools in Sri Lanka, offering classes up to G6-13 in jaffna district. Its education zone is vadamarachchi of karaveddy educational division under northern province. , where 351 students(211 males, 140 females) study.
